Telegram Bot

How to create your Telegram bot:

  1. Open Telegram and search for @BotFather
  2. Send /newbot and follow the prompts (give it any name)
  3. BotFather will give you a token like 123456:ABC-DEF...
  4. Paste that token below and click Save
  5. Open your new bot in Telegram and press Start
  6. The bot will confirm it's connected

Each user uses their own bot. Your token is stored securely and only used to send you flashcard reminders.
